you can do it well, even if you're drunk

This blog explores the necessity of modularizing tech to the point that you can do it: at 100%, drunk, tired, or when you don't have any time.

You really shouldn't have to remember a bunch of stuff, other than the notes you leave for yourself.

Script things whenever possible. Use tools in other people's git repos, dev smart, even if you are in a dum-dum mood (drunk.)

Accomplish your dumb goals quick, and move on to other projects at break-neck mega-pro speeds.
